Membership

Prospective members are invited to contract the club's Secretary (membership@mohawkflyingclub.org) regarding club shares for sale. If an existing club member is interested in selling their membership, the Club will prioritize putting a prospective new member into contact with the prospective seller if desired. Otherwise, a limited number of club-owned memberships are available at a cost of $6,000. Total membership is limited to 15 per aircraft.




Approval

Whether a club membership is purchased or there is a member-to-member sale, the Club must approve the prospective new member. The club will handle the sale proceeds, aircraft checkout and keys, will facilitate access privileges to the aircraft operating area.

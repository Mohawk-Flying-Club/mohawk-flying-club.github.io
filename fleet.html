Fleet

The Club currently offers two high-wing four passenger Cessna aircraft, both with some of the best safety records in the general aviation fleet.




Cessna 172

Our IFR-equipped Cessna 172 is economical to operate and is available for training, local sightseeing, and longer cross countries.




Cessna 182

Our high-performance Cessna 182 is well-suited to longer cross-country travel.




Avionics

Both aircraft have similar instrumentation including Garmin GTN-650 touchscreen nav/comms to simplify transitioning between the aircraft, the C-182 including a digital Garmin 2-axis autopilot.

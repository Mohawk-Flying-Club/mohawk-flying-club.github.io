About

Founded in 1960, Mohawk is a premier member-owned flying club based at Gerald R. Ford International Airport (KGRR) in West Michigan. We currently operate two Cessna aircraft that are hangared at KGRR, and are in the process of adding a Cirrus SR20 to our fleet in 2021. The club emphasizes safety, continuous aircraft improvement, and camaraderie.



People

Our members range from professional jet pilots to newly-minted VFR pilots, who benefit from online scheduling, a maintenance officer, and numerous CFI's. Membership is limited to not more than 15 per aircraft, and currently totals 26 members.

The club emphasizes safety, continuous aircraft improvement, and camaraderie.
